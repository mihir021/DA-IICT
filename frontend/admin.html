ℹ️ How This Model Works — Read Before Training

RECOMMENDED MODEL: XGBoost (Gradient Boosting)

WHY XGBoost FOR RETAIL/E-COMMERCE?
XGBoost performs strongly for e-commerce clickstream forecasting across seasonal patterns, demand velocity, and user behavior. It typically outperforms simple regression and ARIMA for multi-category retail clickstream data.

WHAT DATA DO YOU NEED?
Minimum: 3 months of clickstream events. Ideal: 1+ year to capture seasonal trends and campaign effects.

WHERE TO GET TRAINING DATA?
Option 1: Use the loaded clickstream_events.parquet (10.2M events, 500K users, 5,368 SKUs, Jan-Dec 2024).
Option 2: Upload CSV with: timestamp, user_id, event_type, sku_id, category, price_seen_usd, quantity.

KEY FEATURES:
  • day_of_week, hour_of_day, user_segment, device_type, referral_source, ab_group
  • session_duration_s, scroll_depth_pct, time_on_page_s, price_seen_usd, quantity
  • category, is_mobile, search_query presence
TRAIN / TEST SPLIT: TimeSeriesSplit on timestamp. Train on Jan-Sep 2024, test on Oct-Dec 2024. Never shuffle.

METRIC: RMSLE (target: below 0.5) and AUC-ROC for conversion prediction.
